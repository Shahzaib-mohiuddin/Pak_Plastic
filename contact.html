Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

What is your minimum order quantity?

Minimum order quantities vary by product type. Please contact our sales team for specific MOQ requirements for your desired products.

Do you offer custom manufacturing?

Yes, we specialize in custom plastic solutions. Our engineering team can work with you to develop products that meet your exact specifications.

What are your lead times?

Lead times depend on product complexity and order quantity. Standard products typically ship within 2-4 weeks, while custom products may require 6-8 weeks.

Do you provide samples?

Yes, we provide samples for evaluation. Contact our sales team to discuss your sample requirements and any associated costs.

What quality certifications do you have?

We maintain strict quality standards and are working towards ISO certifications. All products undergo rigorous quality testing before shipment.

Can you ship internationally?

Yes, we export to multiple countries across the GCC region and beyond. We can arrange shipping and handle export documentation.
