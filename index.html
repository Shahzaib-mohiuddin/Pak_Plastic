ABOUT US

Pak Plastic Industry LLC

PAK PLASTIC is a premier Trusted Manufacturing Partner company based in the vibrant city of Umm Al Quwain, United Arab Emirates. With a reputation for excellence and a commitment to delivering top-quality products, PAK PLASTIC has established itself as a trusted name in the industry.

PAK PLASTIC is one of the leading manufacturers and suppliers of plastic crates in the UAE. We offer quality plastic pallets, plastic crates, spill pallets, waste bins, and all kinds of industrial packaging and material handling to meet the needs of customers.
