Revolutionary technology that sets us apart from traditional manufacturing

First in the Region

PAK PLASTIC INDUSTRY L.L.C is proud to be the first company in the UAE region to adopt Electric Injection Molding technology. This revolutionary approach to plastic manufacturing represents a significant leap forward in precision, efficiency, and sustainability.

Our electric injection molding machines use digitally controlled servomotors instead of traditional hydraulic systems, providing unprecedented control over every aspect of the molding process including injection, extrusion, clamping, and ejecting.

Electric Injection Molding Process

Step-by-step overview of our advanced manufacturing process

1

Material Preparation

Raw materials including recycled plastics are prepared and fed into the system with precise temperature control.

2

Heating & Melting

Materials are heated to optimal temperature using energy-efficient heating systems with precise temperature monitoring.

3

Injection

Molten plastic is injected into the mold using digitally controlled servomotors for precise pressure and speed control.

4

Cooling & Solidification

Products cool and solidify in the mold with controlled cooling systems ensuring optimal product properties.

5

Ejection

Finished products are ejected using precise servo-controlled ejection systems, ensuring no damage to the product.

6

Quality Control

Each product undergoes quality inspection with automated systems ensuring consistent quality standards.
