About PAK PLASTIC INDUSTRY L.L.C

Innovation, Sustainability, and Excellence in Plastic Manufacturing

PAK PLASTIC INDUSTRY L.L.C is an innovative plastic manufacturing company founded in 2024. We are the first in the region to adopt Electric Injection Molding technology, significantly enhancing precision and energy efficiency.

Unlike traditional hydraulic machines dependent on hoses, valves, and pumps, our electric machines use digitally controlled servomotors for precise control over injection, extrusion, clamping, and ejecting. This technology enables us to utilize over 70% recycled materials, reinforcing our commitment to sustainability.

This project is established on 100% self-financing of the directors - no loans from banks or financial institutions.

Built on Decades of Industry Expertise

Our family businesses showcase deep-rooted experience in plastic manufacturing:

  • Nafees Plastic Industries - Peshawar, Pakistan (Est. 1991)
  • Nafpack Pvt Ltd - Lahore, Pakistan (Est. 2010)

Managed by three generations, showcasing our deep-rooted experience in plastic manufacturing.

Our Vision

Our vision at PAK PLASTIC INDUSTRY L.L.C is to lead the region in sustainable and innovative plastic manufacturing by leveraging cutting-edge technologies. We aim to set new standards in energy efficiency, precision, and eco-friendly practices, fostering a future where high-quality production and environmental responsibility go hand in hand.

Our Mission

To produce high-quality, energy-efficient plastic products by integrating advanced technologies like Electric Injection Molding. We are committed to sustainability by using recycled materials and reducing energy consumption, ensuring eco-friendly practices. Our focus is on precision, innovation, and meeting customer expectations, positioning us as a trusted leader in the industry.
