Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity?

Our MOQ varies by product type. For standard items, it's typically 500-1000 pieces. For custom products, we can work with smaller quantities starting from 100 pieces.

How long does production take?

Standard products: 7-14 days. Custom products: 2-4 weeks depending on complexity. Rush orders available with 24-48 hour turnaround for urgent needs.

Do you offer custom colors and designs?

Yes! We offer full customization including colors, logos, dimensions, and special features. Our design team can help bring your vision to life.

What materials do you use?

We use high-grade plastics including HDPE, PP, PVC, and recycled materials. All materials are food-safe and meet international quality standards.
